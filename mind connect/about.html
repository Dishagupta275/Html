About MindConnect

About MindConnect

MindConnect is a web-based platform designed to connect individuals seeking mental health support with psychology scholars looking for practical experience. By offering anonymous and convenient therapy sessions, MindConnect creates a safe and accessible space for users to engage with trained scholars, fostering both mental wellness and professional development.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make mental health support accessible and affordable for everyone while providing valuable practical experience for psychology scholars.

Our Vision

We envision a world where mental health support is readily available to all, and where psychology scholars can gain the experience they need to become effective professionals.
